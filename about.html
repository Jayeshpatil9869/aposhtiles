OUR STORY

The story of Aposh stems from the mantra of Quality, Driven by the vision to produce quality products and bring transformation to the cement tiling industry, Aposh TILES was founded by Mr. Ishwardayal Nawal in 1980. After a few years of successful operation, Aposh got recognition from the Government of Maharashtra for the quality of Vintage tiles and their dedication to constantly innovate.

The production of Vintage Tiles with modernised mode of production continues till date, maintaining a legacy and grandeur. The company is a family-run organisation with Mr. Pawan Nawal as Managing Director. Armed with decades worth of experience in the field and an unmatched array of designs, Aposh is a one-stop-shop for customers curating their new homes with its Biggest Portfolio In Tiling Solutions in India.

Aposh has now ventured into the field of Ceramic industry by launching MAGNET CERAMIC through their flagship showroom based at Malegaon, Maharashtra.

In 2008 Aposh introduced avant-garde state-of-the-art, high-end ceramic tiles, which has grown together with the company over the decades. In addition to the entire product range of Aposh, new verticals have been added to the product mix to be offered exclusively through MAGNET CERAMIC. Year after year, Aposh has consistently set higher benchmarks with the team's hard work and persistence. Four decades in the family business, driven by innovation and passion to excel, Aposh's formidable and distinctive product range comes with the guarantee of international quality that appeal to a diverse market segmentation.
