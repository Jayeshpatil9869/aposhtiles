Geometric Cement Tiles

Bold, angular patterns like hexagons, triangles, and 3D cubes created using colored cement. These tiles bring a modern, playful dimension to floors and walls, ideal for accent spaces or feature installations.

Encaustic Pattern Tiles

Hand-poured cement tiles with embedded color and designs. Encaustic tiles are known for their historic patterns, durability, and artisan finish. Great for vintage-inspired or eclectic interiors.

Terrazzo Aposh Tiles

A blend of crushed stone, marble, and quartz set in colored cement. These tiles offer a speckled, luxurious surface—popular in upscale living rooms, hallways, and cafés.
