Plain Clay Tiles

Traditional flat clay tiles with a simple, rectangular design that dates back centuries. These versatile tiles are double-lapped for superior weather protection and are available in natural terracotta, as well as various glazed colors. Perfect for heritage buildings, restoration projects, and classic architectural styles.

Mission Clay Tiles

Barrel-shaped clay tiles featuring alternating concave and convex pieces that create a distinctive wave pattern. These classic Mediterranean-style tiles provide excellent water drainage and ventilation while adding timeless charm to Spanish, Italian, and Mediterranean architectural designs.

explore

Spanish Clay Tiles

S-shaped clay tiles that combine the cap and pan of traditional barrel tiles into a single piece. These iconic tiles create the distinctive rippled appearance associated with Spanish and Southwestern architecture while offering simplified installation compared to two-piece mission tiles.

explore

French Clay Tiles

Lower-profile interlocking clay tiles featuring two prominent flutes or grooves per tile. These distinctive tiles offer superior drainage and enhanced shadow lines, creating elegant roofscapes typical of French country estates and European architecture. Their interlocking design provides excellent weather resistance.

explore

Flat Clay Tiles

Modern interlocking clay tiles with a smooth, sleek appearance. These versatile tiles can be installed in various patterns and are available in multiple colors and finishes. Their contemporary design makes them suitable for both traditional and modern architectural styles, offering clean lines with the natural beauty of clay.

explore

Terracotta Floor Tiles

Traditional clay floor tiles with rich, warm reddish-brown tones and natural variations. These rustic tiles develop a beautiful patina over time and provide excellent thermal mass, keeping spaces cool in summer and warm in winter. Perfect for indoor and covered outdoor spaces with Mediterranean, rustic, or farmhouse aesthetics.

explore

Quarry Clay Tiles

Dense, unglazed clay tiles made from ground minerals and clay fired at high temperatures. These extremely durable tiles are available in earthy red, brown, and gray tones and feature natural slip resistance, making them ideal for high-traffic areas, commercial spaces, and outdoor applications where safety is a concern.

explore

Handmade Clay Tiles

Artisanal clay tiles crafted using traditional methods, featuring unique variations in color, texture, and shape. These premium tiles showcase the skilled craftsmanship of artisans and add authentic character to any space. Ideal for heritage restorations, luxury homes, and projects where distinctive, one-of-a-kind tiles are desired.

explore

Encaustic Clay Tiles

Decorative clay tiles featuring intricate patterns created by inlaying different colored clays before firing. These stunning tiles showcase geometric, floral, or traditional designs that become part of the tile body, not just surface decoration. Perfect for creating statement floors, feature walls, and artistic focal points in any space.

explore

Saltillo Clay Tiles

Traditional Mexican clay floor tiles with distinctive warm orange to reddish-brown tones. These handmade tiles are sun-dried before firing, creating unique variations and a rustic appearance. Their natural warmth and character make them perfect for Southwestern, Spanish Colonial, and Mediterranean-inspired interiors and covered patios.
