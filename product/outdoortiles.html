Porcelain Outdoor Tiles

Highly durable and virtually non-porous, porcelain outdoor tiles withstand extreme temperatures, resist fading, and require minimal maintenance. Available in various textures and designs, including stone and wood looks, they're perfect for patios, walkways, and pool decks.

Natural Stone Outdoor Tiles

Elegant and timeless, natural stone outdoor tiles include granite, limestone, sandstone, and quartzite options. Each piece features unique patterns and colors, creating beautiful, organic outdoor spaces with excellent durability and natural slip resistance.

explore

Slate Outdoor Tiles

Known for their distinctive layered appearance and rich, earthy colors, slate outdoor tiles offer excellent durability and natural slip resistance. These metamorphic rock tiles create sophisticated outdoor spaces with their unique textures and color variations.

explore

Travertine Outdoor Tiles

A type of limestone with a distinctive pitted appearance, travertine outdoor tiles offer a warm, Mediterranean aesthetic. Available in beige, cream, and walnut tones, they stay cool underfoot in hot weather, making them ideal for pool decks and patios.

explore

Concrete Outdoor Tiles

Versatile and durable, concrete outdoor tiles offer modern aesthetics with exceptional strength. Available in various colors, textures, and patterns, they can mimic natural stone or feature contemporary designs, providing cost-effective solutions for patios and walkways.

explore

Brick Outdoor Tiles

Classic and rustic, brick outdoor tiles offer timeless charm and exceptional durability. Available in various colors and arrangements, they create warm, inviting outdoor spaces with excellent wear resistance and a naturally slip-resistant surface.

explore

Wood-Look Outdoor Tiles

Combining the beauty of natural wood with the durability of porcelain, wood-look outdoor tiles resist fading, warping, and rotting. These low-maintenance tiles create warm, inviting outdoor spaces with realistic wood grain textures without the upkeep of real wood.

explore

Quartzite Outdoor Tiles

Extremely hard and durable, quartzite outdoor tiles offer the beauty of marble with superior resistance to scratching and etching. These metamorphic rock tiles feature stunning crystalline appearance and natural variations, perfect for luxury outdoor installations.

explore

Pebble Outdoor Tiles

Featuring small, smooth stones embedded in a flexible mesh backing, pebble outdoor tiles create unique, textured surfaces with excellent drainage and slip resistance. Perfect for pathways, garden features, and spa-like outdoor shower floors.

explore

Terracotta Outdoor Tiles

Traditional clay-based tiles with rich, warm reddish-brown tones, terracotta outdoor tiles create Mediterranean and rustic aesthetics. These porous tiles develop a beautiful patina over time, adding character and charm to patios, walkways, and garden paths.
